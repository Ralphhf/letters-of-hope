Our mission

To make sure no one is forgotten

We exist to restore presence where there is isolation, and dignity where the world sees only a sentence.

Every person is more than the worst thing they've done. Our mission is to write to them like we believe it.
✉

Presence

We break the silence of incarceration with consistent, human correspondence — so no one goes years without hearing their name in kindness.

✦

Dignity

Rooted in faith, we treat every incarcerated person as a child of God worthy of respect, mercy, and hope for what comes next.

♥

Community

We equip volunteers to write with confidence, and we walk beside families and the faithful who refuse to give up on anyone.

What we believe

Faith in action, one envelope at a time

Most of the people we write to are Catholic; some are still finding their way, and all are welcome. We don't require anyone to share our faith to receive a letter — we simply let our faith move us to write. Compassion is the invitation; dignity is the message.
