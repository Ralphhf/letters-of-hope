01

The silence

Isolation is its own sentence

Many incarcerated people — a great number of them Catholic, though not all — spend years without contact from the outside world. No visits. No calls. No mail. A letter says something a system never can: you still matter.

02

The answer

Write. That's the whole ministry.

You don't need the right words — you just need to show up.

STEP 01

Choose someone

Browse people on our Write to Prisoners page — name, facility, and mailing address included.

STEP 02

Write your letter

Introduce yourself. Share an ordinary day, encouragement, a prayer. Kindness beats eloquence.

STEP 03

Mail it safely

Follow the facility's simple mail rules — our FAQ walks you through them — then send it.
